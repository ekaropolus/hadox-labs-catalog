The product thesis

Intelligence should survive scrutiny.

Regional rankings are easy. Decision-grade intelligence is harder: every claim needs provenance, every comparison needs context, and every opportunity needs a path to action.

Mexico Scientific Intelligence explored a repeatable operating system for that harder job. It combined structured state profiles, scientific signals, market context, evidence registries, and report workflows in one product architecture.
